Rye Light Sock

Description

This is a simple, free vanilla sock pattern by Tin Can Knits.

Supplies

  • Fingering Weight Yarn
  • US 1 Needles
  • US 2 Needles
  • Stitch Markers
  • Darning Needles

The Pattern Makes the Following Sizes:

  1. Baby
  2. Toddler
  3. Child
  4. Adult S
  5. Adult M
  6. Adult L
